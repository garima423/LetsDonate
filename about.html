WE ARE NONPROFIT TEAM

The idea behind LetsDONATE is to enable a giving culture between two sets of people - one who want to give and other who want to take. The consequences of poverty are manifold and it impacts families through generations. An intervention at the right time, like educating one generation or providing livelihood to increase income can help families break the cycle of generational poverty.
